# Stay Safe Online – Simple Prevention Checklist
## General Safety
-Use strong passwords and make sure each account has a different one.
-Turn on 2-step verification (2FA) for important accounts like email and social media.
-Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links sent through SMS, email, WhatsApp, or Instagram.
-Always check who the sender is before sharing any personal details.
-Keep your phone, apps, and browser updated to stay protected.
-Avoid logging into important accounts when using public Wi-Fi.
-Always log out if you’re using someone else’s device.
-Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores.
## UPI / Payment Safety
-Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, CVV, or bank passwords with anyone.
-Remember: you don’t need to enter your PIN or scan a QR code to receive money.
-Don’t scan QR codes sent by unknown people.
-Double-check payment requests before approving them.
-Use trusted apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or your bank’s official app.
## Reporting & Help
-If you think something is wrong or you’ve been scammed, report it on the cybercrime portal.
-For urgent financial fraud, call 1930 immediately for help.

# Stay Safe Online – Simple Prevention Checklist
## General Safety
1.Use strong passwords and make sure each account has a different one.
2.Turn on 2-step verification (2FA) for important accounts like email and social media.
3.Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links sent through SMS, email, WhatsApp, or Instagram.
4.Always check who the sender is before sharing any personal details.
5.Keep your phone, apps, and browser updated to stay protected.
6.Avoid logging into important accounts when using public Wi-Fi.
7.Always log out if you’re using someone else’s device.
8.Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores.
## UPI / Payment Safety
1.Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, CVV, or bank passwords with anyone.
2.Remember: you don’t need to enter your PIN or scan a QR code to receive money.
3.Don’t scan QR codes sent by unknown people.
4.Double-check payment requests before approving them.
5.Use trusted apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or your bank’s official app.
## Reporting & Help
1.If you think something is wrong or you’ve been scammed, report it on the cybercrime portal.
2.For urgent financial fraud, call 1930 immediately for help.

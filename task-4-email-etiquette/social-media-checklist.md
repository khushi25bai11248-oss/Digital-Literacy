# Social Media Do’s & Don’ts Checklist
## Do’s
Be kind and respectful when you post or comment.
Keep your account private or adjust settings to protect your personal information.
Share content that is useful, positive, or meaningful.
Always double-check information before posting or forwarding it.
Try to maintain a clean and professional image online.
## Don’ts
Don’t share personal details like your phone number, address, or passwords publicly.
Avoid posting anything offensive, rude, or inappropriate.
Don’t spread fake news or unverified information.
Stay away from cyberbullying or making negative comments about others.
Don’t overshare every detail of your personal life online.
